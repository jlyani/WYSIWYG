About Us


Who We Are

Happy Cup Cafe is a cozy cafe designed for students and young professionals who need a comfortable space to study, relax, or hang out with friends. We serve quality drinks and snacks at prices that are easy on the budget.

What We Offer

We serve a variety of hot and cold beverages, from classic brewed coffee to iced matcha latte. We also have freshly baked pastries and snacks to go with your drink. Our menu is simple but made with care.

Our Location

We are located in a quiet neighborhood, easy to find and easy to love. Whether you drop by for 30 minutes or spend the whole afternoon, you are always welcome here.
